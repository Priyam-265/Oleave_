Frequently Asked Questions

How do I place an order?

To place an order, browse our menu, select your items, and click on "Add to Cart." Once you've finished selecting your items, go to your cart, and proceed with the checkout process.

How do I track my order?

After placing your order, you can track it through our website by logging into your account and navigating to the 'My Orders' section.

What payment methods do you accept?

We accept all major credit cards, PayPal, and Apple Pay. You can choose your preferred payment method at checkout.
