Partner Onboarding

I want to partner my restaurant with Oleave +

To partner your restaurant with Oleave, please fill out the partnership form on our website. You will need to provide basic details about your restaurant and agree to the terms and conditions.

What are the mandatory documents needed to list my restaurant on Oleave? +

The mandatory documents required include a valid business license, proof of ownership or lease agreement, food safety certificates, and a menu with pricing.

I want to opt out from Google Reserve +

To opt out from Google Reserve integration, you can contact our support team via the contact form or email us directly at support@oleave.com.

After I submit all documents, how long will it take to go live on Oleave? +

Once all documents are submitted and verified, it typically takes 7-10 business days to go live on the Oleave platform.

What is this one-time onboarding fee? Do I have to pay it while registering? +

The one-time onboarding fee covers the setup and integration of your restaurant into the Oleave platform. It is typically paid during registration or when you sign the partnership agreement.

Whom should I contact if I need help and support in getting onboarded? +

If you need help with onboarding, please reach out to our support team at support@oleave.com or contact your onboarding manager directly.

How much commission will I be charged by Oleave? +

The commission charged by Oleave is typically a percentage of each transaction processed through the platform. Please refer to the partnership agreement for specific rates based on your restaurant's location and size.
